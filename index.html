About Us

Legacy Affordable Pro Movers is a dedicated and renowned logistics company in California. Our business specializes in making sure that all your moving needs are attended to with the best team of honest, hardworking and reliable people. We offer a vast range of household, corporate, and commercial moving services on both local and long distance basis. We go above and beyond your expectations to deliver on time and with a smile thereby making your move easy and stress free. We provide trucks(on demand), dollies, blankets and shrink wrap(to cover the furniture), straps as well as all the tools needed to disassemble and reassemble the furniture and packing services. We also do move-out cleaning(on demand). We are Licensed and Insured and our services come to you at an affordable rate. Call us today to make a reservation for your move. We get the job done, take all moving stress off you and we ensure that we put smile on your face with the quality of our services.
